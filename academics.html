    A Curriculum Built for Every Learner

    At Mbopha Secondary School, we utilize a streaming system to ensure every learner is placed in an academic path that matches their strengths, interests, and future career goals. While streams diverge based on specialization, all learners share a foundation of three compulsory subjects.

    Compulsory Subjects

    English First Additional Language IsiZulu Home Language Life Orientation

    Grade 8 and Grade 9 Subjects

    All Grade 8 and Grade 9 learners follow the Senior Phase curriculum prescribed by the Department of Basic Education. Learners study the following compulsory subjects:

    Subject Description
    English Home Language Language, literature, writing and communication skills
    isiZulu First Additional Language Additional language proficiency and communication
    Mathematics Number, algebra, geometry and data handling
    Natural Sciences Basic concepts in Physical Sciences and Life Sciences
    Social Sciences Geography and History
    Economic and Management Sciences (EMS) Business, entrepreneurship and financial literacy
    Technology Design processes, innovation and practical problem-solving
    Creative Arts Visual Arts, Drama, Dance and Music appreciation
    Life Orientation Personal development, health, citizenship and physical education

    Promotion Requirements

    To be promoted from Grade 8 to Grade 9 and from Grade 9 to Grade 10, learners must satisfy the promotion requirements prescribed by the Department of Basic Education, including:

    • 50% in Home Language
    • 40% in First Additional Language
    • 40% in Mathematics
    • 40% in three other subjects
    • 30% in two remaining subjects

    Our Academic Streams

    All streams: 7 compulsory subjects + 1 Elective subject = 8 subjects total
    Base subjects for all: English, Zulu, Life Orientation

    1. Tourism Stream

    Compulsory: Geography, Mathematical Literacy, Life Sciences

    Elective – Choose 1: Creative Arts or Drama

    2. Commerce Stream

    Compulsory: Business Studies, Economics, Accounting + Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy

    Elective – Choose 1: Tourism or Creative Arts or Drama

    3. Physical Science / Maths Stream

    Compulsory: Physical Sciences, Mathematics, Life Sciences + choose 1 from: Accounting, Agricultural Sciences or Geography

    Elective – Choose 1: Tourism or Drama or Creative Arts

    Note: Note: CAT was removed. Learners now choose another elective.

    4. History / General Stream

    Compulsory: Life Sciences, Geography, History, Mathematical Literacy

    Elective – Choose 1: Drama or Creative Arts

    5. Agricultural Science Stream

    Compulsory: Agricultural Sciences, Life Sciences, Mathematics, Geography

    Elective – Choose 1: Creative Arts or Tourism or Drama

    💡 New: Creative Arts, Drama and Tourism now available as an 8th subject selection

    Assessment & Examinations

    Mbopha Secondary School adheres strictly to the National Senior Certificate (NSC) requirements. Our assessment strategy involves rigorous continuous assessment throughout the year, culminating in formal examinations.

    We prepare our Grade 12 learners not just to pass their matric examinations, but to achieve university-level entrance results.
